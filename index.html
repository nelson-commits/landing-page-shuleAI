Our Story

Born from a real classroom problem

In 2021, our founder Collins Mwangi discovered how kids are addicted to games and thought — why not create games that actually nurture students educationally, instead of games like Minecraft and Roblox that don't add academic value? That thought became Shule AI Plus+, guided by teachers who provided curriculum materials and direction.

Collins teamed up with his long-time friend Anthony Wai, a software engineer, and together they built the first prototype: a single CBC-aligned game tested at Marurui Primary School and Royal Brains Kinderstart School.

"Now imagine a whole curriculum for your kid in a game that he or she can actually play on your phone…"
— Collins Mwangi, CEO & Founder

Today, Shule AI Plus+ serves over 12,000 learners across Kenya — from urban Nairobi to rural Turkana — with 10K+ games covering all 12+ CBC subjects, AI-powered hints in both English and Kiswahili, and real-time parent dashboards.
